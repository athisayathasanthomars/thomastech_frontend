Personal Data and Privacy Protection in Online Learning

Why Privacy Matters in E-Learning?

Our E-Learning platform ensures the highest level of data protection for students enrolled in Software Development and Data Science courses.

Types of Data Collected

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing Information (if applicable)
  • Course Progress and Assessment Data

How We Protect Your Data?

We implement strict security measures such as encryption, secure login authentication, and data access controls.

Legal Compliance in Sri Lanka

We comply with Sri Lanka’s data protection laws, ensuring your privacy is safeguarded.

  • **Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022** – Regulates the processing of personal data and enforces data security.
  • **Computer Crimes Act No. 24 of 2007** – Criminalizes unauthorized access to personal information and cybercrimes.
  • **Right to Information Act No. 12 of 2016** – Ensures transparency in data collection and user rights to access information.

Student Responsibilities

Students should protect our course videos,records and lession notes and avoid sharing sensitive data, and report any suspicious activities.
